Meet the Owner

My Name is
Isaiah Parkesui

"I grew up herding cattle across these plains. Now I share them with the world — not as a tourist attraction, but as a living home."

Born in a Maasai village in the Amboseli region, Isaiahstudied tourism at the Wildlife Clubs of Kenya and spent years as a guide before founding his own company — driven by a simple idea: that authentic travel should benefit the people who call these places home.

When you travel with us, you're funding school fees, livelihoods, and a future for the Kimana community. Isaiah employs only local guides. Every shilling stays local.

Terms & Conditions

Reservations, Payment & Pricing

A deposit is required to hold your reservation. The remaining balance is due in Kenya Shillings (cash or M-Pesa, or equivalent in USD, Euros, or GBP) upon pick-up on the start date of your tour. Tours not paid in full upon pick-up will be cancelled and deposits forfeited.

Cancellation & Refund Policy

Cancellations must be made in writing via email. In the event of cancellation — before or after the start date, or failure to arrive — deposits paid will be forfeited. No partial refunds are issued for early departures.

Payment of Deposits

We accept M-Pesa, wire transfer, PayPal, or Western Union. Additional fees from payment providers are the responsibility of the traveller.

Accommodations & Vehicles

We recommend a maximum of 3 people per vehicle for comfort. A 2nd vehicle is required for 5+ people; a 3rd for 9+ people. Accommodations are subject to availability.

Medical & Accessibility

Please advise us of any medical conditions prior to booking. Most tours are not wheelchair-accessible. We cannot accommodate women past their 6th month of pregnancy or service animals.

Not Included

Passports, visas, travel insurance, baggage fees, alcoholic drinks, laundry, and gratuities are not included in quoted rates.

Travel Insurance

Travel insurance including medical evacuation and luggage protection is strongly recommended.
